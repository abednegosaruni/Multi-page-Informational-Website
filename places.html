Top Places to Visit

Nairobi National Park

This is the only national park in the world located within a capital city. It is home to lions, rhinos, giraffes, and many bird species. Visitors can enjoy game drives and see wildlife with the city skyline in the background.

Giraffe Centre

The Giraffe Centre is a conservation center focused on protecting endangered giraffes.

Visitors can feed giraffes and learn about wildlife conservation efforts.

Karen Blixen Museum

This museum was once the home of Danish author Karen Blixen, famous for the book "Out of Africa".

It provides insight into colonial history and Kenyan heritage.

Westgate & Sarit Centre

These are popular shopping malls offering a variety of stores, restaurants, and entertainment options.
