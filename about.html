About Nairobi

History

Nairobi started as a railway depot in 1899 during the construction of the Uganda Railway.Over time, it grew into a major administrative and commercial center.

Today, Nairobi is one of Africa's most influential cities, hosting international organizations like the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP).

Culture

The city is a mix of cultures, languages and traditions from all over Kenya.You will find a blend of modern and traditional lifestyles. Music, food , and fashion in Nairobi reflect both local heritage and global trends.

Economy & Lifestyle

Nairobi uis known as the "Silicon Savannah" because of its growing tech industry. Many startups and tech companies are based here.

The city also offers a vibrant lifestyle with entertainmet spots, malls, restaurants, and parks.
